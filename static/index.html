About Me

Meet Roswitha

Hi! My name is Roswitha, and I’m a 10-year-old artist. I love drawing, making crafts, and turning my ideas into colorful creations. I started my magnet business because I enjoy making art that people can cherish and see every day on their fridge or office desk. Each magnet is made with creativity, care, and lots of fun. My goal is to make little pieces of art that bring smiles to people’s faces. Thank you for supporting my small business!
